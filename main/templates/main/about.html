About KofmanCo

Founded at age 15 by Zachary Kofman, KofmanCo creates AI-powered apps like PricePilot that help investors forecast markets and make smarter investment decisions.

About KofmanCo Founded by Zachary Kofman, KofmanCo is a technology company dedicated to building AI powered tools that guide investors, businesses, and developers to make smarter, faster decisions. What started as a small project quickly evolved into a advanced software company backed by remarkable performance. In just a few months, KofmanCo’s forecasting app, PricePilot, helped generate consistent returns through self made machine learning techniques including LSTM and Facebook Prophet. At KofmanCo, we use our technical skills to build tools that are fast, easy to use, and built to last. Whether it’s for financial forecasting, boosting productivity, or automating data, everything we create is designed to be scalable and user friendly. Our Mission To accelerate opportunity through technology by giving individuals and companies the tools they need to unlock better decisions, deeper insights, and long-term growth. We’re just getting started, and we’re excited to keep building tools that make life easier and results stronger.
